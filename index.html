About Us

What we are

SaiRam under the direct inspiration and guidance of Sri Sathya Sai Baba,an institution,enriched with love and devotion is serving the needy and poor,in the name of SRI SAIBABA GURUKULAM.The main objective of the institution is "SERVICE TO MANKIND IS SERVICE TO GOD".

Sri Sai Baba Gurukulam was started in the year 1988 by two persons with the strong desire to serve the society.Education is the doorway to lead a successsful life,as it is the greatest gift that one can offer to anybody.Thus these two persons decicated their lives to mould the future generation.

Elementary School.Currently there are about 160students on roll.These students are provided with free textbook,notebooks,uniforms and other study materials and including tution fee.

Free tuition Apart from elementary school,Sai Baba Gurukulam also provides free tuition for Xth&+2The children of Gurukulam are from a lower status in the society,both in terms of economic and social condition.It is Gurukulam,that opened the gateway for these children to have a better future.The passed-out students stand well in their life with respect,self-satisfaction and confidence.
